How to Play 🎮

🎯 Basic Controls

  1. Click and drag the mud pie to aim your throw
  2. Pull back further to increase power (watch the power bar change color)
  3. Release to launch the mud pie
  4. Try to throw as far as possible!
  5. Use "Play Again" to keep trying or "Return to Menu" to go back

⚙️ Game Features

  1. Physics Simulation: Realistic gravity and projectile motion
  2. Dynamic Wind: Wind conditions change throughout each throw - pay attention to the wind indicator!
  3. Real-time Stopwatch: Tracks how long your mud pie stays in the air
  4. Distance Tracker: See your current distance as the mud pie flies
  5. Score Tracking: Your highest score and last run are saved automatically

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

SPACE Play again after a throw
R Return to menu
M Toggle music on/off

💡 Pro Tips

  1. Watch the wind indicator - adjust your angle based on wind direction
  2. A 45-degree angle typically gives the best distance in calm conditions
  3. Stronger wind can significantly affect your throw
  4. Practice different power levels to find what works best
  5. Your scores are saved in the browser - see if you can beat your personal best!
